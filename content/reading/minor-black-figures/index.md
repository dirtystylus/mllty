At times the veil between the character of Wyeth and Brandon Taylor’s ruminations on his [newsletter](https://blgtylr.substack.com) gets very thin. Taylor’s interests bubble up: film photography, finely-crafted notebooks and writing implements, foreign films. The ruminations on how to create art driven by intrinsic desire — as opposed to market demand — is intertwined Taylor’s long-running analyisis of the interaction between art and identity politics.
