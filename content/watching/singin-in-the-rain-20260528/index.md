Took Em to see this, she was delighted when the pre-show roll included the [“Duck and Cover”](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IKqXu-5jw60&pp=ygUOZHVjayBhbmQgY292ZXI%3D) info video featuring Bert the Turtle, which she had remixed for a school project years ago.

Watched this as part of the [BMFI](https://letterboxd.com/brynmawrfilm/) 100th anniversary celebration. It was a packed house, and we applauded after the big numbers. Donald O’Connor’s “Make ’Em Laugh” and the two Cyd Charisse numbers at the end were the highlights for me this time around, but I love this movie top to bottom.
