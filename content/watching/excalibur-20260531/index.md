The new Arrow 4K looks incredible, from the opening battle scene: all backlit smoke and fire…the whole film reminded me several times of Coppola’s _Bram Stoker’s Dracula_ in terms of sumptuous visuals. The cast is full of recognizable stars, captured in a moment of youth (I didn’t recognize Ciarán Hinds until late).

The line readings in the early going — as Arthur ascends to the throne — are clunky and over the top (I still have no idea what movie Nicol Williamson is acting in, his Merlin is full of slapstick humor that brings attention to the silliness of the whole thing instead of giving you permission to go along with it.) But the second half finds its footing as dread and death enter the picture.

Green light glowing on polished armor, a rider galloping across smoke and fog, doom everywhere.
