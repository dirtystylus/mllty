Felt like throwback to the late 90s when everyone was copying Tarantino — the pop-culture digressions, the ironic needle drops, the hyper-stylized violence. Vince Vaughn times two is 1.5 too many Vaughns, but it is always fun to see Keith David, Eiza González, and James Marsden. Fun enough but made me look up whether we’re ever going to get a 4K release of Doug Liman’s [_Go_](https://letterboxd.com/film/go/).
