I decided that I should finally watch this before revisiting Ridley Scott’s _Kingdom of Heaven_, and I felt the thematic kinship between the two — both strip away the romance of the sword and armor, and look at how religion, patriarchy, and monarchy crush the people within their systems. I imagine this is an even tougher watch now than when it originally came out.

Great casting, top to bottom. Matt Damon makes the most of his famously punchable face, just a great portrayal of a guy who is the biggest pill in any room despite being mostly in the right. Adam Driver plays a version of a medieval high-school jock, someone whose charm keeps propelling him into ever-higher circles of privilege and power. Jodie Comer needs more roles like this — she really sells the turn into horror-movie vibes in the final third. Nobody is having more fun than Ben Affleck, though, who steals every scene he’s in.
