Such a pleasure to revisit this. The titles really tell you this was in the _Minority Report_/_Bourne Identity_ era.

What a cast! Jarring to see a very young Ken Leung, just a week after I finally finished season 4 of _Industry_. Clive Owen was white-hot in 2006, which also gave us _Children of Men_. And of course Denzel makes it all look so easy, even as he moves through the different emotional, political, and ethical decisions his character makes.

I love a heist film, but beyond the mechanics of the robbery-not-robbery I appreciated how the motivating force was a desire for justice and reckoning, something in such short supply today.
