Richard Gere as Jesse reminded me of Tony from _Looking for Mr. Goodbar_: sleazy, horny, and dangerous. Jesse is so off-putting at first that I almost bailed on the film. I’m glad I stuck it through because you can feel this movie’s influence on some of the pop-culture obsessed 90s films (Tarantino’s come to mind for the Silver Surfer bits alone). _Gun Crazy_ as the projected backdrop to sex? Just pure text.
