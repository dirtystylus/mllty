Watched this over two nights with our ballerina S. Evoked spontaneous cries from both of us at the incredible athleticism, and also laughter at how choreographers communicate the ineffable through gesture and wordless sound.

I appreciated how Wiseman intercut the rehearsal/dance footage with quieter moments featuring the dozens of staff who work on maintenance, food service, costumes, sets…it really felt like a small city unto itself.
