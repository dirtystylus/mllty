Absurdist, at times slapstick, comedy. Vibes from bribes. The gags counterbalance the dread that emerges as the characters gradually reveal what the regime has taken from them — until that balance collapses and you’re left with an ending that feels like it’s suspended in the air, with no clue which way things will land. A final scene that hits like a punch yet remains ambiguous.
