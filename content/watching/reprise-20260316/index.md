Finally worked my way to the start of the Oslo trilogy. There is a lot of formal experimentation going on here, and you can see some of the techniques that get refined later on in _Oslo, August 31st_ and _The Worst Person in the World_: the voiceover, the novelistic chapter stops, the needle drops. I felt the influence of French New Wave films, _Vertigo_ (that Paris sequence is excruciating), and _Run Lola Run_. It doesn’t all add up but it is nice to see Eskil Vogt and Joachim Trier figuring out their storytelling style.
