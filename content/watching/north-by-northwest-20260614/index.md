70mm projection at Bryn Mawr Film Institute. Played great to a sold-out crowd, so many laughs at the one-liners and double entendres. Cary Grant and Eva Marie Saint on a big screen — we used to have capital-M Movie Stars.

I’ve seen this so many times and yet my palms still get sweaty at the Mount Rushmore scene. The final cuts — from the monument to the sleeper car to the train entering the tunnel…just giggle-inducing pure cinema.
